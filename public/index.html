How to Play

The game aims to improve your memory.
There are 2 signals which you must remember. They are the position of the light and the letter pronounced.
In a game of 2-back, you must press the position matching button ("a" on the keyboard by default)
if the position in the current round is the same as 2 rounds ago.
Press the audio matching button ("l") if the letter you heard in this round is the same as 2 rounds ago.
In a game of 3-back, press the matching button if the signal is the same as 3 rounds ago, and so on.
This may be disorienting at first, but if you keep trying, you will succeed and your memory will improve.
